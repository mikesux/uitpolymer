UIT FAQ

What is UIT?

UIT is a 1 year startup immersion program that develops startup founders using project-based courses, an open-source curriculum and proven entrepreneurs as mentors. The program is designed to teach innovative business techniques, concentrating on 3 topics: technology, business and product development.

What kind of people are you looking for?

We are looking for enthusiastic aspiring founders, willing to take chances and immerse themselves in the world of tech startups. If you are someone who wants to learn about how to launch a startup or become an aspiring entrepreneur who is keen or has already started a business and want to learn more, we want to talk with you!

What am I going to learn at UIT?

UIT teaches aspiring founders to code, create effective designs, pitch ideas, attract co-founders, raise money, recruit a team, and develop a company. Our hands-on experience covers the intense exploration and hard decisions startup founders face when launching a startup business. We’ll show you how founders of startups think about growth and take action at an accelerated pace.

How do I receive accreditation?

UIT has worked closely with Cape Breton University to make sure the projects meet the requirements of select courses from the CBU course calendar. 45 credits will be earned for fully completing the UIT program.

What is the class size?

For 2015-2016 admission year, we will be accepting 20 UIT aspiring founders to our startup immersion. We'll increase that number each year after.

Why UIT? I could get full accreditation for Computer Science somewhere else?

UIT isn’t just a school; it is full startup immersion. The technical, business and product development skills you learn here will work together to give you a competitive edge over other startups all over the world. Everything you learn at UIT is useful in today’s economy.

Do I need to bring a laptop?

No. We will supply you with a laptop and software.

Can I receive financial aid?

Yes. UIT is a recognized program within the Shannon School of business and Cape Breton University. For details on financial aid services, burseries and scholarships please visit: CBU Financial Services or contact us at: contact@uitstartup.org

Is food & lodging covered?

No. There is an option to apply to live at Cape Breton University Residence. If you are moving to Sydney to attend UIT and looking for lodging, our team can help you with arranging a place to live and with any questions you may have about Sydney. We are here to help make you feel comfortable and ready to work!

Should I commit to anything else during UIT?

No. It is not advised that you attempt to balance anything else while attending UIT. It is a completely immersive 11 month startup experience and should take precedence over almost everything!

What is the admission process?

Complete the application on the UITstartup.org. Once application is completed, we will contact you with the next step.

Where is UIT located?

UIT is located in downtown Sydney, Nova Scotia, Canada. Sydney is a port city on Canada’s beautiful East Coast. It is in the province of Nova Scotia on scenic Cape Breton Island. UIT building is located in the heart of downtown Sydney, offering walking distance to restaurants, shopping and Sydney Harbour boardwalk. For more information on Sydney visit: capebretonisland.com and cbisland.com

Do I have to be in Sydney, Nova Scotia?

Yes. You will need to attend the UIT building.

Do you have lectures? Are they open for auditing?

Yes. We have numerous guest speakers from a variety of concentrations speak on the topics you are currently learning. If you wish to attend a UIT lecture please email us at contact@uitstartup.org

Will I attend CBU?

Yes. Each semester will have one course that is relevant to the topics you are learning at UIT. You will be required to go to the CBU Campus for this course.

Can I receive a tour of UIT?

Yes. We love showing off the UIT facility and answering any questions you may have about the UIT experience. To book a one on one tour please contact us at: contact@uitstartup.org

What do I need to know as an International Student?

If you are an international applicant considering UIT, please contact us to learn more about student requirements and tuition at contact@uitstartup.org. Please note that if you are recommended for admission to UIT it can take a few months to obtain your student visa. Please ensure that you apply early enough in the admission period to leave enough time to obtain your Visa and arrive in Canada before UIT begins.

Can I register for Health Insurance?

Yes, you can apply to be covered under the health insurance plan at CBU.
