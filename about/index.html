ABOUT UIT

UIT is a 1 year startup immersion program that develops founders using project-based courses, an open-source curriculum and proven entrepreneurs as mentors. The program is designed to teach innovative business techniques, concentrating on 3 topics: technology, business and product development to help aspiring entrepreneurs understand how the worlds most successful startups have achieved their goals. UIT teaches aspiring founders to code, create effective designs, pitch ideas, attract co-founders, raise money, recruit a team, and develop a company.

UIT is an extension of CBU, giving the students an opportunity to earn university credits for their efforts. The pilot UIT program will have 10 students, who will work over 11 months towards 45 available university credits. 15 total courses, 5 courses per semester, 3 credits per course.

The UIT facility is an innovative, exciting and vibrant startup work environment. Lab stations and old classrooms have been removed and replaced with an open concept workplace with all the amenities you would find in an actual startup firm. You’ll find whiteboards for conceptualizing, TVs and projection screens for pitching ideas, and comfortable workstations for developing your product. UIT consists of an interactive, modern, work space located downtown Sydney, Nova Scotia. Aspiring founders have 24 hour access to the secured downtown UIT immersion.

OUR OBJECTIVES

Promote innovation, collaboration and commercialization through project based courses, open-source curriculum and entrepreneurship mentorship

Address the seed-stage investment gap, empowering novice aspiring entrepreneurs to become co-founders

Provide educational opportunities and training tools for entrepreneurs

Showcase entrepreneurship assets and infrastructure locally, nationally and internationally

Who Is This Course For?

Looking For A Career Path

Would you like to work for a big company or work for yourself? Do you wonder what life will be like if you take the long road and try to make your ideas a reality? We specialize in taking your ideas and helping them grow and develop.

Aspiring Founders

You are already starting or keen to start a business. By the end of UIT Startup Immersion, you'll have brought your idea to life, gained the confidence, ressources and new training to progress at far greater speed.
