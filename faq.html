FAQ

What is UIT?

UIT is a startup immersion program that teaches students the methods behind world's most successful startups. Through the 11-month program, students learn technology and business skills leading up to their final project: the launch of theif own product!

What kind of students are you looking for?

The students accepted into the program are based on an interest in technology and entrepreneurship, and aptitude for self-directed learning, and an ambition to create a career in the technology startup world.

What am I going to learn at UIT?

You will learn how to create your own startup. From product to technical development.

How do I receive accreditation?

UIT has worked closely with Cape Breton University to make sure the projects meet the requirements of select courses from the CBU course calendar. 45 credits will be earned for fulling completing the UIT program.

What is the class size?

For the first year (2014-2015) we admitted only 13 students (6 female, 7 male). We'll increase that number each year after.

Why UIT? I could get full accreditation for Computer Science somewhere else.

UIT isn’t just a school; it is full startup immersion. The technical, business and product development skills you learn here will work together to give you a competitive edge over other startups all over the world. Everything you learn at UIT is useful in today’s economy.

Do I need to bring a laptop?

No. We will supply you with laptop and software.

Is food & lodging covered?

No. But you can apply to live in the dorms at CBU..

Should I commit to anything else during UIT?

No. It is not advised that you attempt to balance anything else while attending UIT. It is a completely immersive startup experience and should take precedence over almost everything!

What is the admission process?

Complete the application on the UITstartup.org.

Do I have to be in Cape Breton?

Yes. You will need to attend the UIT building.

Do you have lectures? Are they open for auditing?

Yes. We will have guest speakers from a variety of concentrations, speak to the topics the students are currently learning. If you wish to attend a UIT lecture please email us at contact@uitstartup.org.

Will I have to go to CBU?

Yes. Each semester will have one course, each semester that will require you to go to the CBU Campus.

Can I register for Health Insurance?

Yes, you can apply to be covered under the health insurance plan at CBU.
